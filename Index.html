Chanda Aam (1/16, 6.25%) applies to Non-Musi members. Hissa Aamad under Al-Wasiyyat replaces Chanda Aam for Musi members, at a minimum of 1/10th, rising to 1/3rd depending on the individual's pledge and means. Chanda Jalsa Salana (1/120, 0.83%) is payable by all earning members alongside either of the above. Khuddam and Ansar Membri Chanda (1% of income) is a separate auxiliary-organization contribution, on top of your main Jama'at chanda, if you're a member of that auxiliary. Ijtema Chanda is a further, separate one-time annual donation collected for the Khuddam-ul-Ahmadiyya or Ansarullah Ijtema — its amount is fixed each year by the local/national Majlis rather than calculated as a percentage of income, so enter the figure announced to you. Other funds (e.g. Tehrik-e-Jadid, Waqf-e-Jadid) are not included. For an authoritative figure, confirm with your local Jama'at or auxiliary Finance Secretary.
